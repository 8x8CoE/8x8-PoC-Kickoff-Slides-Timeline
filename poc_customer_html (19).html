❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is the goal of this PoC?+
The goal is to give your team a hands-on opportunity to evaluate the 8x8 platform in your own environment — validating how it supports your use cases and success criteria before any long-term commitment.
What is expected from our team?+
Your participation is key — attending kickoff, orientation, and wrap-up sessions, actively using the platform during testing, and providing feedback so we can accurately validate outcomes.
What happens after the PoC is complete?+
We review results against the agreed success criteria together and align on next steps — whether that's deployment, refining requirements, or exploring additional capabilities.
Who do I contact if I have an issue?+
Use the shared project inbox on the Contacts tab — it reaches the whole 8x8 team instantly. You can also raise a ticket via support.8x8.com.
How are success criteria validated?+
Collaboratively during Phase 2 — your team runs agreed test scenarios and both parties confirm each criterion as met. Track live progress on the Overview tab.
